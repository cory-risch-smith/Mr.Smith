ABOUT

Envision Design was created through the consideration of how to make Design more accessible and all inclusive. When thinking about the population of those who experience Color Vision Deficiency (CVD), or any degree of colorblindness. “Colour (color) blindness (colour vision deficiency, or CVD) affects approximately 1 in 12 men (8%) and 1 in 200 women in the world. In Britain this means that there are approximately 3 million colour blind people (about 4.5% of the entire population), most of whom are male.”(https://www.colourblindawareness.org/). Inspired to find a way to advocate and provide resources for those seeking to create more inclusive design, to find resources and access materials, Envision Design was born. Envision Design simultaneously serves, both as a beacon, and as a pillar for Designers with CVD or any degree of colorblindness. It provides a space for Designers to care, to question, and to collaborate. It is an expansive space that encourages innovation, creation, but is rooted in selflessness and service.
